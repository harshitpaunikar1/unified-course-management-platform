# Unified Course Management Platform

> **Domain:** EdTech

## Overview

Institutes and training businesses juggle multiple tools to run courses, slowing teams and frustrating learners. Enrolments handled manually, content scattered across drives, schedules, assessments, and certifications lack a single source of truth. Instructors have little visibility into learner progress or at-risk cohorts, while operations spend hours reconciling data for reports. Without a unified platform, launch timelines slip, support tickets spike, and leadership cannot act on real-time insights. The result: higher acquisition costs, lower completion rates, compliance risks, and lost revenue from cancellations and drop-offs.

## Approach

- Mapped workflows with admins, instructors, learners; captured pain points and success metrics; prioritised MVP aligned to business goals
- Designed scalable data model for courses, modules, sessions, users, enrolments, assessments, certifications, payments; defined audit trails and retention
- Architected modular web app with role-based access, secure APIs, content authoring; integrated calendar, notifications, payments
- Built assessment engine (quizzes, assignments, rubrics), gradebook, attendance; enabled cohorts, waitlists, prerequisites, automated certificate issuance
- Instrumented analytics for engagement, completion, cohort health; added alerts for at-risk learners and A/B tests for content
- Executed QA with automated tests, accessibility checks, load testing; ran sprint demos, UAT, phased rollout, training
